About Me

Hello, I'm Jacob Simper. I am from Tooele Utah but currently living in Peck Idaho (middle of nowhere northern Idaho). I have lived here for a little over a year. My family moved while I was serving my mission in South Carolina. I work in construction, helping build my grandparents home and figured I do pathways at the same time before I eventually go to BYU-I. I also like listening to audio books, board games, volleyball, spike ball, and all that kind of thing too.
